# By The Brew — Menu with Detailed Attributes

> **Note:** This document provides structured attributes for every item on the By The Brew menu. Use it for filtering, recommendation, allergen lookup, and dietary suitability matching.
> All milk-based coffees can be made vegan with Oat or Almond milk (+₹60).
> "Not Enough Data" lists attributes that cannot be inferred reliably from the source recipe doc and should be confirmed with staff (e.g., exact calories, exact sugar grams, sourcing of cocoa, specific brand of syrups).

## BEVERAGES — COFFEES

### Espresso
- **Category:** Coffee (Hot)
- **Serving:** Hot only, ~30 ml shot
- **Temperature:** Hot
- **Taste:** Bold, intense, slightly bitter, rich aroma
- **Ingredients:** Freshly ground espresso beans, water
- **Contains:** Caffeine
- **Does Not Contain:** Milk, sugar, dairy, gluten, nuts, eggs
- **Possible Allergens:** None typical
- **Sweetness:** None (unsweetened by default)
- **Spice Level:** None
- **Body:** Full-bodied, concentrated
- **Caffeine Level:** High (single shot)
- **Dietary:** Vegetarian, Vegan, Gluten-Free, Dairy-Free
- **Sugar Source:** None
- **Customization:** Add sugar on request; cannot be made decaf in-house (confirm with staff)
- **Avoid If:** Caffeine-sensitive, pregnant (limit caffeine), have acid reflux/GERD, on caffeine-restricted medication
- **Good For:** Morning kickstart, post-meal digestif, quick energy boost, purist coffee lovers
- **Not Enough Data:** Exact bean origin/roast profile, calorie count, decaf availability
