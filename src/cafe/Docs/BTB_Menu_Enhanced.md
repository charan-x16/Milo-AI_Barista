# By The Brew — Complete Cafe Menu

> **Currency:** All prices in Indian Rupees (₹ / INR)
> **Taxes:** Extra as applicable
> **Preparation:** All orders freshly prepared — may take time
> **Milk Upgrades:** Any coffee drink can be upgraded with plant-based milk for ₹60 extra (Oat / Almond)
> **Vegan Note:** Items tagged **Vegan** contain no animal-derived ingredients. Milk-based coffees (Latte, Cappuccino, etc.) can be made vegan by upgrading to Oat or Almond milk (+₹60) — ask staff to confirm. Items tagged Vegetarian but not Vegan contain dairy, eggs, honey, or other animal products.
> **Serving Size — Drinks:** 350 ml (coffees, cold brews, cold coffees, mocktails, iced teas, shakes) · 275 ml (herbal teas, hot chocolate)
> **All Pizzas:** 10 inches, thin crust
