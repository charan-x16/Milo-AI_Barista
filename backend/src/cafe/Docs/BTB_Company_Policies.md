- All prices listed on the menu are in Indian Rupees (₹ / INR).
- Prices are exclusive of applicable taxes. GST and other government-mandated taxes will be added to the final bill as per current regulations.
- Where two prices are shown for beverages (e.g., ₹189/₹209), the first price is for the Hot variant and the second is for the Iced variant.

### Bill & Invoice

## Order & Preparation Policies

### Order Placement
- Orders can be placed at the counter, through our in-store digital kiosks (where available), or via supported online ordering and delivery platforms.
- All orders are subject to availability of ingredients and items. In the rare event an item is unavailable, the customer will be informed immediately and offered alternatives.
- Modifications and special requests (e.g., less sugar, no ice, extra spicy) are accommodated where possible. Some modifications may not be feasible due to the nature of the recipe.

### Preparation & Wait Times
- All orders are prepared fresh and from scratch. Estimated preparation times are as follows:
  - **Espresso-based drinks:** 2–5 minutes
  - **Cold brews:** 2–3 minutes (pre-steeped, served fresh)
  - **Shakes & cold coffees:** 4–6 minutes
  - **Herbal teas:** 5–8 minutes
  - **Mocktails:** 3–5 minutes
  - **Appetizers & sides:** 8–15 minutes
  - **Sandwiches & wraps:** 10–18 minutes
  - **Salads:** 8–12 minutes
  - **Pastas:** 18–25 minutes
  - **Pizzas:** 15–25 minutes
- Wait times may be longer during peak hours, weekends, and holidays. We appreciate your patience.
- If an order is taking significantly longer than expected, customers are encouraged to check with the counter staff.

### Order Accuracy
- We take every effort to ensure order accuracy. Customers are encouraged to verify their order at the time of billing and when receiving their food/drinks.
- If an incorrect item is served, please notify the staff immediately. We will replace the item at no additional cost.
- Orders cannot be modified or cancelled once preparation has begun, except in cases of allergen concerns or safety issues.

## Return, Refund & Cancellation Policies

### General Return & Replacement Policy
- **Freshly prepared items cannot be returned for a refund once served and accepted by the customer**, as all food and beverages are made to order and are perishable in nature.
- However, By The Brew is committed to customer satisfaction. If you are unhappy with your order, please bring it to our attention immediately (within 5 minutes of receiving the item), and we will do our best to resolve the issue.

### Grounds for Replacement (No Additional Charge)
By The Brew will replace an item free of charge in the following circumstances:
- **Wrong item served:** An item different from what was ordered is served.
- **Quality issue:** The item is visibly stale, undercooked, overcooked, or does not meet our quality standards.
- **Foreign object:** A foreign object is found in the food or drink.
- **Allergen error:** An allergen that was specifically communicated to the staff at the time of ordering is present in the item due to staff error.
- **Temperature issue:** A hot drink is served cold (or vice versa) contrary to the order.
- **Missing components:** A part of the order is missing (e.g., a side, a topping, or a drink).

### Grounds for Refund
- Refunds are issued only in exceptional circumstances and at the discretion of the store manager.
- A full refund will be processed if:
  - An item cannot be replaced due to ingredient unavailability.
  - The customer has a verified allergic reaction caused by a staff error in allergen communication.
  - A significant and unresolvable quality issue is identified.
- Refunds will be processed via the original payment method within 5–7 business days.
- Cash payments will be refunded in cash at the counter.
- Card and UPI refunds will be processed digitally and may take 5–7 business days to reflect in the customer's account depending on their bank.

### Non-Refundable Situations
- Change of mind after the order has been prepared and served.
- Dislike of taste or personal flavor preference (e.g., "too bitter", "not sweet enough") — we will try to adjust the item if raised immediately, but this does not qualify for a refund.
- Items consumed more than 50% before a complaint is raised.
- Delay in service during peak hours (we will try to prioritize, but delays are not grounds for a refund).
- Items ordered through third-party delivery platforms — refund and return policies of the respective platform will apply.

### Cancellation Policy
- **Dine-in orders:** Orders can be cancelled free of charge if the cancellation is made before preparation begins. Once preparation has started, the full amount will be charged.
- **Online/delivery orders:** Cancellation is allowed within 2 minutes of order placement or before the order is accepted by the kitchen, whichever is earlier. After that, the full amount will be charged.
- **Pre-orders and catering:** Cancellations must be made at least 24 hours before the scheduled time. Cancellations within 24 hours may incur a 50% cancellation fee. No-shows are non-refundable.

## Food Safety & Hygiene Policies

### Kitchen & Food Safety Standards
- By The Brew operates under strict FSSAI (Food Safety and Standards Authority of India) guidelines and holds a valid FSSAI license.
- All kitchen staff are trained in food safety, personal hygiene, and safe food handling practices.
- Temperature-controlled storage is maintained for all perishable ingredients — dairy products, meats, and fresh produce are stored at or below 4°C; frozen items at -18°C or below.
- Deep fryer oil is changed regularly as per schedule and never reused beyond safe limits.
- All food contact surfaces, utensils, and equipment are cleaned and sanitized after every use and at regular intervals throughout the day.
- Handwashing is mandatory for all kitchen and service staff before handling food, after breaks, and after handling raw ingredients.

### Ingredient Freshness
- Coffee beans are freshly roasted and sourced from premium suppliers. We aim to use beans within 2–4 weeks of roasting for optimal flavor.
- Milk, cream, and dairy products are sourced from reputable brands and checked for expiry before each use.
- Vegetables, fruits, and fresh produce are procured daily or every other day to ensure maximum freshness.
- Pre-packaged ingredients (syrups, sauces, chocolates) are checked for seal integrity and expiry dates before use.

### Pest Control
- Professional pest control services are engaged on a regular schedule (monthly or as required) for all outlet premises.
- Pest control records are maintained and available for inspection.

## Allergen & Dietary Policies

### Allergen Information
- By The Brew takes food allergies seriously. Our menu items may contain or come into contact with the following major allergens:
  - **Milk/Dairy** (present in most coffee drinks, shakes, cheese-based items, cream sauces, paneer, butter)
  - **Wheat/Gluten** (present in bread, pizza bases, tortillas, pasta, flour-based batters, cookies)
  - **Eggs** (present in some batters, mayonnaise, ice cream, certain baked goods)
  - **Tree Nuts** (present in hazelnut syrup, Nutella, pine nuts in pesto, almond milk)
  - **Soy** (present in soy milk, some chocolate products, Biscoff spread, soy sauce)
  - **Fish** (present in traditional Caesar dressing which contains anchovies)
- Customers with food allergies must inform the staff at the time of placing their order. Our team will do their best to accommodate and advise on safe options.
- **Cross-contamination risk:** Our kitchen handles multiple allergens in a shared environment. While we take precautions, we cannot guarantee a 100% allergen-free preparation for any item. Customers with severe or life-threatening allergies should exercise caution.
- Allergen information for each item is available in our detailed menu and recipe documentation. Customers may request this information from the staff.

### Dietary Accommodations
- **Vegetarian options:** Clearly marked on the menu. Our vegetarian items are prepared with care, but may be cooked using shared equipment with non-vegetarian items.
- **Vegan-adaptable options:** Many coffee drinks can be made vegan by substituting dairy milk with plant-based milk (Soya, Oat, or Almond at +₹60). Some food items may also be adaptable — please ask the staff.
- **Caffeine-free options:** All herbal teas (Blue Pea, Chamomile, Lavender, Hibiscus, Apple Cinnamon) and all mocktails are caffeine-free.
- **Gluten-free options:** Salads (without croutons), grilled chicken with veggies, and most beverages are naturally gluten-free. Please confirm with staff for specific items.
- **Jain diet:** Some items may be adaptable (no onion, no garlic) — please discuss with the staff at the time of ordering.
- **Halal:** By The Brew sources chicken from reputable suppliers. Customers requiring halal certification should confirm with the specific outlet.

### Nutritional Information
- Approximate nutritional data (calories, protein, carbohydrates, fat, sugar, fiber, sodium, caffeine) is available for all menu items in our detailed recipe and nutrition documentation.
- Nutritional values are estimates based on standard recipes and portion sizes. Actual values may vary depending on preparation, customization, and ingredient batch variations.
- Customers with specific nutritional requirements (e.g., calorie counting, diabetic diets, low-sodium diets) are encouraged to consult with the staff for guidance.

- By The Brew collects personal data (such as name, phone number, email address) only when voluntarily provided by the customer, for example during loyalty program enrollment, online orders, catering bookings, feedback submissions, or Wi-Fi login.

## Sustainability & Environmental Policies

### Waste Reduction
- By The Brew actively works to minimize food waste through accurate demand forecasting, optimized portion sizes, and efficient inventory management.
- Unsold food that is still safe for consumption may be donated to local food banks or partner NGOs where logistically feasible.
- Food waste that cannot be donated is disposed of responsibly through composting or authorized waste management services.

### Packaging
- We use eco-friendly, biodegradable, or recyclable packaging for takeaway orders wherever possible.
- We encourage customers to bring their own reusable cups and containers. A small discount may be offered at select outlets for orders served in customer-provided reusable cups.
- Single-use plastic straws are not provided. Paper straws or reusable straw options are available on request.

### Energy & Resource Conservation
- Energy-efficient equipment (espresso machines, refrigerators, lighting) is used wherever feasible.
- Water conservation practices are followed in the kitchen, including efficient dishwashing procedures and minimizing water waste.
- Staff are trained to turn off equipment and lights when not in use.

### Responsible Sourcing
- Coffee beans are sourced from reputable farms and suppliers who follow ethical and sustainable growing practices.
- We prioritize suppliers who adhere to fair labor practices and environmentally responsible methods.
- Where possible, we source local and seasonal produce to reduce our carbon footprint.

## Feedback, Complaints & Grievance Resolution

### How to Reach Us
- **In-store:** Speak to the outlet manager or fill out a feedback form.
- **Email:** feedback@bythebrew.in (responses within 48 hours)

### Complaint Resolution Process
1. **Acknowledgment:** Every complaint is acknowledged within 24 hours (in-store complaints are addressed immediately).
2. **Investigation:** The relevant team investigates the complaint, gathering details from staff, CCTV footage (if applicable), and order records.
3. **Resolution:** A resolution (replacement, refund, apology, process improvement) is communicated to the customer within 48–72 hours.
4. **Escalation:** If the customer is not satisfied with the resolution, the complaint can be escalated to the regional manager or the central customer experience team.
5. **Closure:** The complaint is formally closed once the customer confirms satisfaction or after the escalation process is complete.

### Feedback Culture
- We actively encourage feedback — positive or constructive. It helps us improve.
- Customers who provide regular feedback through our loyalty program or feedback channels may receive thank-you rewards or early access to new menu items.
